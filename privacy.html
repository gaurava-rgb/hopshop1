  • Prevent abuse, secure our systems, and comply with legal obligations.

Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Sharing and processors

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate agreements.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service, resolve issues, comply with legal requirements, and protect our rights. Message logs may be kept for troubleshooting and security, then deleted or anonymized.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
